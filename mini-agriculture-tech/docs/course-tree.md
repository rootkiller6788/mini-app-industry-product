# Course Dependency Tree — mini-agriculture-tech

## Prerequisites for this Module

```
Fundamentals
├── C Programming (struct, pointer, memory management)
├── Numerical Methods (floating-point, trig functions)
├── Statistics (probability distributions, stochastic processes)
└── Linear Algebra (coordinate geometry)

Domain Knowledge
├── Soil Science (texture classes, pedotransfer functions)
│   └── Rawls et al. (1982) — Soil Science Society of America
├── Crop Physiology (phenology, RUE, harvest index)
│   └── DSSAT/APSIM documentation
├── Meteorology (Penman-Monteith, GDD, solar geometry)
│   └── FAO-56, Allen et al. (1998)
├── Hydrology (curve number, runoff, drainage)
│   └── USDA NRCS TR-55, National Engineering Handbook Part 630
├── Soil Chemistry (nitrogen cycle, CEC, pH)
│   └── Stanford & Smith (1972), Godwin & Jones (1991)
├── Environmental Science (erosion, water quality, GHG)
│   └── USLE/RUSLE, IPCC Guidelines
├── Agricultural Economics (break-even, ROI, insurance)
│   └── Kay, Edwards & Duffy (2016), Goodwin & Smith (2014)
└── Plant Pathology (pest epidemiology, mycotoxin risk)
    └── Zadoks & Schein (1979), EFSA (2020)

Optional Advanced Prerequisites
├── Machine Learning (k-means clustering for VRT zones)
├── Optimization (greedy, LP/ILP for rotation planning)
├── GIS (coordinate systems, spatial interpolation)
└── Parallel Computing (GPU-accelerated field simulation)
```
